CV

Education

I have an MA in Painting from the Hungarian University of Fine Arts. My master was Dóra Maurer. From 2010 to 2013 I took the Phd program at the Hungarian University of Fine Arts Doctorate School. My supervisor was Zoltán Szegedy-Maszák. Closely related to my Phd research (“Groupmachine – social dreamwork as Art”) I have received my psychodrama assistant degree in 2018 from the Hungarian Psychodrama Association. Languages: english, french - intermediate exam.

Prizes, scholarships, residencies
